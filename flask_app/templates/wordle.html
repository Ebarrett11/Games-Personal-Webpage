How to play

This game plays similar to Wordle. The difference is that in this version, the length of the word can be different. If the word is 8 letters long then you will have 8 tries to guess the word. You can guess a word using the on screen keyboard or your own keyboard. If it is a real word then it will be accepted. If the tile turns gray then that letter is not anywhere in the word, yellow means that letter is in the word just in the wrong location, and green means that it is the correct letter in the correct place.
